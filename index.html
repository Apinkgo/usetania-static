Keep Track of Your Farm
in One Place

TANIA is a free & open source farmer journal to help you
manage your farm easily by accessing information
you need on-the-go.

download (x64)
or build Tania from its source by yourself. Go to our GitHub.

Current version 1.7.0. Code licensed Apache 2.0

Why Tania is Good for Developers and Farmers?

built for iot

Thinking about connecting climate sensors and irrigation controllers and be able to run it remotely when you're out-of-town? We have your back. Tania is built for IoT lovers like you!

extensible

Extensible modules to help your Tania system meet your needs. You can create your own standard operational procedures that suits to your farm business.

open source

Tania will always be a free and open source software to give you the freedom to manage your farm the way you like it. It's available on GitHub.

Tania is collective-community driven

Tania is an open source project hosted on GitHub, originated by Tanibox in 2017. The Tania community
of dedicated developers, users, farmers, researchers and agriculture experts made it possible.
To contribute, share knowledge and experiences, you can also join us on:

  • GitHub

  • Telegram
